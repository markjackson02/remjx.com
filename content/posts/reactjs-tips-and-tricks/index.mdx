[React.js](https://reactjs.org/) is my front-end framework of choice. Here are some tips, tricks, and articles I have found useful:

- [React Higher-Order Components in Typescript](https://medium.com/@jrwebdev/react-higher-order-component-patterns-in-typescript-42278f7590fb) by James Ravenscroft. Higher order components are used to wrap components in a reusable manner. Covers "Enhancers" and "Injectors".
